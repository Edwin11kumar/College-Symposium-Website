Event Description

Adhere to event guidelines for a smooth experience. Respectful behavior and compliance ensure an enjoyable occasion.

Prezentare

Join our Electronics and Communications Presentation Event! Individuals or teams of 3 can showcase their work in a 15-minute slot, emphasizing key points and creativity. Discuss sustainability and ethics for responsible innovation.

SuperNova

Experience the Electronics Quiz Challenge! From a General Knowledge warm-up to a Rapid Fire balloon activity round, culminating in the intense Buzzer Round, embark on a thrilling journey of knowledge and fun to claim the quiz champion title!

Clash Of Circuits

In "Circuit Quest," teams answer 20 electronic circuit questions in 180 seconds each. The top-scoring team advances. "Circuit Debugging" requires fixing malfunctioning circuits within a time limit. "ElectraSim" uses TinkerCad for efficient circuit simulation, determining winners based on performance and time.

Classroom Premier League

Experience our unique cricket format with underarm techniques, keeping bowlers' arms below hip level. Wides grant extra runs, fielders catch above waist height. Teams get 2 overs, earn singles by hitting the wall, while direct wall hits or aiming for six result in outs. Boundaries score four runs, adding excitement to each over.

Cinematic War

Join our dynamic Tamil Music and Cinema Quiz for teams of 2-3. Decode Tamil songs in 'Guess the Song,' identify BGMs in 'BGMI,' and connect clues in 'Connexions.' Dive into this exciting contest of musical and cinematic knowledge!

Mind Marathon

Explore the 'Mind Marathon' contest for teams of 2-3. Start with the 'Picture Puzzle,' test memory in 'The Twisted Tongue Twister,' and analyze images in 'Picture Perception.' Sharpen your minds in this thrilling journey of memory, speech, and perception!
